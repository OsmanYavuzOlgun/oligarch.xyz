OLIGARCH

Oligarch is an exclusive organisation that brings together highly driven individuals with unique skill sets who are determined to succeed and achieve their goals. As a community, we believe that by connecting and collaborating, we can achieve our goals easier. Oligarch provides opportunity, vision, and unity to its members, allowing them to rise with respect and dignity.

Lore

The Oligarch was created to protect and use Erumá, a magical sword forged in ancient times. War has begun, Knights march into the light.

The organization maintained balance until enemies united and marched upon The Oligarch to obtain Erumá. To fight the war, The Oligarch summoned 99 knights from all corners of the medieval world, and victory was won by their sacrifice.

The knights never returned. Recently, 99 coffins were discovered in the cellar of a castle. Only individuals with the necessary qualifications can open these coffins, and The Oligarch awaits its new knights.
